A place to talk about menopause.

We want to create a site that supports women before, during and after the menopause. A site which is not filled with dry, impersonal, medical facts but which shares expert advice, authentic voices, interviews, fashion and is funny, frank and kind

We started this because people need it, there is nothing else like this out there, and we know from first hand experience that many women donâ€™t know what to expect, feel alone and feel unrepresented in the media

We have already spoken to over fifty women at all stages of the menopause and they have spoken about feeling embarrassed and alone. They have used words like ‘loss’ and ‘taboo’. In almost every case they have been surprised by what are very common symptoms. We want to change this.

What’s the name all about?

The menopause is often described - shamefacedly and elliptically as ‘the change’ or ‘the time of life’.

We want to embrace the change. This is often a time when you pause and consider your life, when you are at the peak of your earning potential or are in a powerful position.

We want to turn the embarrassment round and challenge the taboos that often come with the menopause.

We don’t want you to have the ‘time of life’. We want you to have the time of your life.

Why have we invited you here?

You have been invited here because we would like you to be one of the founding contributors.

We hope that you recognise that the site will be stylish, well designed and will be a place for irreverent, supportive, fresh and philosophical content that will help millions of women have the time of the lives.
